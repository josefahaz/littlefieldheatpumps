## How to Upload a New Photo

### Step 1: Prepare Your Photo
- Make sure your photo is in JPG, PNG, or GIF format
- Photo should be less than 5MB in size
- For best results, use photos that are at least 800x600 pixels

### Step 2: Fill Out the Upload Form

1. **Photo Title** - Give your photo a descriptive title
   - Example: "Residential Heat Pump Installation - Brunswick"
   
2. **Category** - Choose which gallery this photo belongs to:
   - **Heat Pump Installations** - For new installation projects
   - **Maintenance & Repair** - For service and repair work
   - **Natural Gas & Propane** - For gas heating system work

3. **Description** (Optional) - Add details about the project
   - Example: "Mini-split heat pump installation in a 2,000 sq ft home"

4. **Select Photo** - Click "Choose File" and select your photo

5. **Preview** - You'll see a preview of your photo before uploading

6. **Click "Upload Photo"** - Your photo will be uploaded to the website

## Managing Your Photos

### Viewing Photos
- All uploaded photos are organized by category
- Scroll down to see photos in each gallery section

### Editing a Photo
1. Find the photo you want to edit
2. Click the **"Edit"** button
3. Enter a new title or description
4. Click OK to save changes

### Deleting a Photo
1. Find the photo you want to remove
2. Click the **"Delete"** button
3. Confirm you want to delete (this cannot be undone!)
4. The photo will be permanently removed

## Tips for Great Photos

✅ **DO:**
- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Show the completed work from multiple angles
- Include photos of both indoor and outdoor units
- Take photos of clean, finished installations

❌ **DON'T:**
- Upload blurry or dark photos
- Include customer faces without permission
- Upload photos with personal information visible
- Use photos larger than 5MB (compress them first if needed)

## Troubleshooting

### "File size exceeds 5MB limit"
- Your photo is too large
- Use a photo compression tool or resize the image
- Most smartphones can resize photos in the gallery app

### "Please select an image file"
- Make sure you're selecting a JPG, PNG, or GIF file
- PDF files and documents won't work

### "Upload failed"
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page and uploading again

### Photos not appearing on website
- Wait a few seconds and refresh the gallery page
- Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+F5 on Windows)
- Make sure the upload said "Photo uploaded successfully!"
